Welcome to the Crystal Game!

How To Play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below and by clicking on one you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number and you lose if you go over. The value of each crystal is hidden and each time the game restarts, the values of each crystal will change.
